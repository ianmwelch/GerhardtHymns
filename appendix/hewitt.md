In his book, *Paul Gerhardt as a Hymn Writer and His Influence on English Hymnody*, [Theodore Hewitt](/authors/hewitt_t) provides extended commentary on ten of Gerhardt’s more popular hymns in English and American hymnals. Provided here are those commentaries. The entire book may be read online at the [Christian Classics Ethereal Library](https://www.ccel.org/ccel/hewitt/gerhardt.html).
